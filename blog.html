Love lasts Forever
Post By: KenShi's Studio
07-23-2022

This was by far the most heart wrenching moment of my photography career. A lovely old couple came to the shop to take photos together. While I was taking their pictures, they told me all about their life's story; how they met, their family and much more. I even cried as they said to me, "We want to take atleast one last photo of us together before our passing". It was truly an unforgetful experience.

About Studio

KenShi's Studio was made on the 30th of November 2021 and is founded by owner; Marshelle Anne F. Domingo.

The studio aspire to capture the beautiful things in life of families, friends and more! Ever since the owner was a kid, she always had a little toy camera everywhere she went, she would pretend to take pictures and show them to the people she took pictures of. For her, it was really fun! Even as she grew she loved the idea of capturing special moments with the help of a camera, and because of this studio, her mere dream, came to life.
